# Privacy Policy — योजनालाभ

**Last updated:** June 2026

## Overview
योजनालाभ ("the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how it is used.

## Data We Collect

### Information You Provide
The App asks you to enter the following information to check scheme eligibility:
- Age
- Occupation
- Caste category
- Gender
- State of residence
- Annual income (approximate range)
- Land ownership (approximate acres)
- BPL card status
- Marital status
- Girl child status
- Disability status

**This data is stored only on your device** in a local SQLite database. It is never sent to any server, cloud, or third party.

### Automatically Collected Data
- **AdMob:** The App uses Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID) and usage data to serve relevant ads. See Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### No Account Required
The App does not require you to create an account, provide your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information.

## How We Use Your Data
- Your profile information is used **only on your device** to calculate eligibility percentages for government schemes.
- No data is transmitted, sold, or shared with any third party (except standard AdMob ad serving).

## Data Security
All data remains on your device. The App does not have any server backend. You can clear all data by uninstalling the App or clearing App data in Android Settings.

## Third-Party Services
- **Google AdMob** — serves advertisements. Privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- **GitHub** — scheme update data is fetched from a public GitHub repository (no personal data is included in these requests)

## Children's Privacy
The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal data, contact us to have it removed.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page.
