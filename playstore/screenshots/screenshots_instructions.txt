# Screenshots Instructions

## Play Store Requirements:
- **Phone screenshots:** 2-8 required (min 1080 x 1920 px, 3200 x 3840 px max)
- **Tablet screenshots:** 0-8 optional (min 1080 x 1920 px)
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG

## How to take screenshots from your phone:

### Method 1: Install APK on phone and capture
1. Copy `build/app/outputs/flutter-apk/app-release.apk` to your phone
2. Install it
3. Open the app → take screenshots at each screen:
   - **Screenshot 1:** Profile form (with data filled in) — show Hindi UI
   - **Screenshot 2:** Results screen showing eligible schemes with percentages
   - **Screenshot 3:** Scheme detail screen showing full description

### Taking screenshots on Android:
- **Most phones:** Press Power + Volume Down simultaneously
- **Samsung:** Press Power + Volume Down or swipe palm across screen
- **MIUI/Xiaomi:** Power + Volume Down or three-finger swipe down

### Transfer screenshots to PC:
1. Connect phone via USB cable
2. Enable File Transfer on phone notification
3. Copy from `DCIM/Screenshots` folder to this directory
